Accumulated prize pool/Jackpots
The accumulated prize pool or commonly known as jackpot is one of the main features of slots. The prize is not easy to win, but the winning amount is huge. Basically, any player that takes part in this has to wager a certain amount of money and will be added into the total prize pool. If a person wins, he or she can take home the prize money that reaches up to 1 million or more.
Multi-payline
In traditional casino slots, a person will only win if the three icons in the middle line are identical. However, in most online slots, you can pay extra to activate more paylines. For example, if 5 identical icons are formed in 3 lines or diagonal lines, you win. The latest online casino games have even more lines beyond 5 thus further increasing your win chance.
Game of multiples
You win more as you put more coins in the game of multiples. Every coin is paid according to the ratio, meaning, if the ratio is set to be 10:1, then you are paid 50 coins if you inserted 5 coins.
