What are odds in football (soccer)?
Before starting your football bets, it’s important to learn and understand how sports betting odds work. Sports betting odds are usually set by a football bookie, while the odds for online football bets are determined by the betting website. However, the sports betting odds provided varies to different casinos, thus it is the best to keep betting on sites you familiar with.There are two types of sports betting odds:

1. Fractional Odds
Fractional odds are presented with two numbers divided by a slash, for example, 2/1, 3/1, and 2/3. How the sports event will likely end can be determined through simple calculation from the fractional odds.

2. Decimal Odds
Another type of sports betting odds is in decimal point form. Some bettors may find the decimal odds easier to comprehend compared to fractional odds. Both sports betting odds are equally good. It all comes down to the preference of the bettors at the end of the day. Sports betting odds are just a reference for you to know how much money you can earn when you win your bet.

What are the Best strategies for soccer betting?
Football betting is the most popular gambling games, commonly for the younger players due to their interest. While horse betting is commonly for the older players. Even so, your successes in online football betting rely heavily on your football betting strategies. Here are some of the best football betting strategies as analyzed from various online sportsbooks at the follwing FAQ:
Bet on Both Teams to Score.
It means that if both of the teams you bet on score within the time limit, you win. However, it does not always come down to the two teams. Factors such as smooth passes and key passes, stronger offensive strategies or weaker defensive strategies, will contribute to the successful scoring of each team. The best way to evaluate the scoring potential of both teams is to compare the scoring styles of the forwards and the defenders, as well as a precise assessment of the previous goals and scores.
Double Chance Bets.
The nature of football shows that there is no guarantee that one team will always win especially when it involved real money. The worst bet choice could turn out to be impactful and comes out on top, while the well-performed teams might lose. Therefore, doubling the bets could be one of the best strategies in football betting. Double Chance Bets means that you can bet on the match results whether it will end in a draw or one team winning and don’t have to pick which is the winning side. It helps to reduce the risk in a game when there is no obvious side to bet on.
Draw and Weaker Team Bets.
If you are looking for valuable bets in football odds, then you should start developing a habit of value betting and not betting on the teams with higher winning chances. The teams with higher winning chances may be able to win a lot of games, but the odds are very low, and thus, winning are low as well. The best football betting option are on betting draws or the weaker teams.
Half Time/Full Time (HT/FT) Bets.
Try Bets on the team that you think will win at half time, full-time or a tie. Sportsbooks usually provide multiple football betting options with high odds which help to ease your decision-making process while you predict the outcomes in half time and full time.
Handicap Bets
This betting option is considered to be very risky but good payout. For instance, if you bet on your team with 1 point handicap, it means that your team has to win the match by two points.
Accumulator Bets
This is another risky bet as above. Two or more individual bets are combined in a single bet which aims to achieve a remunerative profit from a small bet. The idea is to accumulate the value of the bet. However, this will only win when all the bets are spot on. If you lose either bet, then whole bet is lost. Keep in mind when you are making this risk.
